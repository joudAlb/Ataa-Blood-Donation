important tips:

1-Start by booking a suitable blood donation appointment.

2-Drink plenty of fluids the day of your donation.

3-Get a good night's sleep the night before.

4-Bring a list of all the medications you are taking.

5-Eat a healthy meal before your donation.

6-Eat a snack to help your blood sugar levels return to normal.

7-Avoid strenuous exercise for the rest of the day.

8-Monitor the needle insertion site for any signs of bleeding or infection.
